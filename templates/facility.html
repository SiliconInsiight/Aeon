OUR FACILITIES

CAMP FIRE

It creates a wonderful ritual that carries serious memory power: sitting around a campfire will become the natural ending to the day’s events. Whether you’ve just risen from that superb, summertime barbeque, or everyone’s home from the scattered schedules of camp or athletics, the fire brings everyone together and in a circle. Often, when we ask our kids for their favorite holiday memories, “sitting around the campfire” leads the list.

SPORTS & RECREATIONAL FACILITIES

Whether to soak in the pool, sun bathe or sit by the pool side and enjoy a cocktail, the pool is a favorite location among guests. The sunken Pool Bar offers you an opportunity to enjoy a drink while relaxing in the pool. We also have a kids' pool where youngsters can enjoy themselves in safety. Swim suite is compulsory to use the pool.

CONFERENCE FACILITIES

We believe that relaxing, fun and inspiring environs contribute towards developing better corporate strategies. We have an impressive, ethnic looking conference hall, "THE EMERALD". It is equipped with state-of-the-art audio visual facilities to conduct conferences, meetings or corporate retreats. The Emerald is an air-conditioned conference hall capable of accommodating up to 250 persons.

SATELLITE TV

We provide a range of cable TV channels via Satellite. These include BBC, HBO, ESPN, Star Sports, National Geographic as well as Indian TV channels. Although the last thing you should do at Poovar Island is spend your time in your room watching TV, we provide this feature as we want to ensure your complete satisfaction.

LAUNDRY SERVICES

We provide a complete laundry service from washing and pressing to dry cleaning. We offer both a normal and express service. Laundry collected before 10.00 am from your room will be delivered on the same day at normal rates. Laundry collected after 10.00 am and requested for the same day will be charged 50% extra on our normal rates.
